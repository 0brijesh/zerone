Case Study:

"EdTech - Offline To Online in 3 months"

Problem Statement

*EdduKart is a well know education brand, having served more than 10,000 students in Delhi. Unfortunately when COVID 19 pandemic hit across the world including India, the entire EdduKart’s business came to a standstill and it became a do or die situation. That’s when Eddukart’s management took a strategic decision to move the entire education business online. Eddukart team approached Zerone Software Labz to help them build a solution which could help them achieve their vision in the shortest possible time.

Objectives

  • To develop a solution that's compatible across platforms including android and IOS considering the diversity of devices used by it's students
  • To build solutions that's fast to scale and develop to mitigate the increasing competition from the rise of education technology companies
  • To develop the solution in the shortest possible time

Approach & Solution

Agile Methodology

Agile methodology made a lot of sense as the client was still trying to figure out lot of things and requirements were not frozen. We did a competitor analysis along with some primary research of the target audience, to prioritize and select the user stories that seemed most relevant for the MVP. The Agile methodology as well helped us to establish a learning feedback loop with the students, using which we could continue the development effort in the right direction.

Technology

Considering the requirement to have a solution compatible across the devices & shortage of time, Flutter & Firebase was the best suited technology, using which we could develop solution for android and IOS with a single code base. Thus saving a lot of engineering time, cost and flexibility to easily experiment with new ideas.

Transition

Students were gradually onboarded on the platform in a chunk of few 100 users and with in a matter of few months, all the students were successfully registered and were easily able to commence online classes.

Results

  • Flutter was a game-changer for Eddukart, allowing them to continuously scale without sacrificing quality.
  • EdduKart build time was significantly faster by using Flutter and we were able to launch the MVP in just 3 months.
  • EdduKart was able to retain and continue imparting education to 70% of the existing student base.
  • The application became a strategic add on for growth of business and venture in to online education for long term.
